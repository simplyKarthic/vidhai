Empowering Recycling: Our Inspiring Journey.

‘Two Technocrats’ inspired by a subject called Recycling in 8th semester of college, set out to start the own pyrolysis unit (waste plastics to oil) after quitting a high pay job and leaving the dream of scientist to rest.

  • A Journey of Inspiration and Transformation

    Two Technocrats' pursuit of a sustainable recycling solution driven by passion.

  • Pioneering Sustainable Recycling:ViDHAI TRADERS

    Empowering Recycling for a Greener Future Championing recycling through the purchase and sale of diverse scrap materials.

As destiny had better plans, it dropped us exactly at the doorstep of Kodungaiyur dumpyard for our first encounter with wastes. From then, the seed of thought and quench to find/discover a sustainable recycling solution was sowed. After 6 months of grassroot study, we registered our company as ‘ViDHAI TRADERS’ who will buy and sell all kinds of scrap.

F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

Discover answers to commonly asked questions about our recycling services, processes, accepted materials, and environmental benefits.
  • What types of plastics do you accept for recycling?

    We accept a wide range of plastics for recycling, including PET, HDPE, PVC, LDPE, PP, and PS.

  • How can I prepare my plastics for recycling?

    It is recommended to rinse out the plastics to remove any residue, remove caps and labels if possible, and separate different types of plastics if required.

  • Do you provide recycling services for individuals or only businesses?

    We provide recycling services for both individuals and businesses. Everyone can contribute to a greener future!

  • Where can I find your drop-off locations for recycling?

    You can find our drop-off locations on our website's "Locations" page. We have conveniently placed collection points across various areas.

  • What happens to the plastics after they are recycled?

    After recycling, the plastics go through a process of cleaning, sorting, and processing. They are then transformed into new products, reducing the need for virgin materials and promoting a circular economy.

  • What are the environmental benefits of recycling plastics?

    Recycling plastics helps conserve natural resources, reduces energy consumption, decreases greenhouse gas emissions, and minimizes landfill waste, contributing to a cleaner and more sustainable environment.
